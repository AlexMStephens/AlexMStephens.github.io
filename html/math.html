I do mathematics.

[2023-12-26 Tue]

I am a PhD student in the Mathematics program at the University of Manitoba, under the supervision of Dr. Raphaël Clouâtre. I earned by BSc in mathematics at the University of Winnipeg in 2020, and my MSc in mathematics at the University of Manitoba in 2022.

My main interests fall under the umbrella of "mathematical analysis", particularly operator theory and non-commutative convexity.

~Alex Stephens
